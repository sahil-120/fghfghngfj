SCHOOL RULES AND REGULATIONS

Every student should carry his/her Almanac to Academy every day. Any remarks from the Educator or the Principal should be shown on the same day to the parents/guardian, who must sign under it to indicate that they have read the same.

Students should reach Academy on time every day.

The warning bell before a classes or assembly is a signal for all to go to the assembly or to their respective class rooms. This should be done promptly and in silence.

should be habitually clean and always neatly dressed. They should cut their nails regularly and polish their shoes daily. Students who are not in proper uniform will not be allowed to enter their class rooms.

The students must take care of all the school properties and no student is allowed to spoil the desks or chairs or board or draw anything on the wall or in any way damage things belonging others Damage done even by accidents should be reported at once to Grade Educator or Principal.

The students who come to the Academy by their own transport should reach at least Ten minutes before the Assembly.

The students should observe polite manner wherever they go, they should always remember that they should greet their Educators and any visitors to the school when they meet them. Bullying and use of foul language are punishable offence.

The Academy reserves the right to terminate the studies of students with unsatisfactory progress in studies or whose conduct is a bad influence on other.

The name of grade and section of the pupil should be clearly marked on all belonging of the students. Blazer, coats and jerseys should bear the name of the student.

The Students are not allowed to bring motorcycle to Academy.

The Academy is not responsible for articles/personal belongings lost by the students.

Students suffering from any infectious disease are not allowed to attend class.

No student is allowed to leave the class or Academy premises during the class hours without the written permission of the Grade Educator and the Principal.

Any visitor to the Academy must enter via reception, where their record will be maintained.

Parents should not enter classrooms to see their wards or educators during Academy hours without permission.

No chewing gum is allowed at the Academy.
